What is RECO2

Reco2 is your handy mobility emissions calculator!
The name stands for "Review" and "CO2". This is a student project by students at the DHBW Mosbach. With our application you can easily and quickly enter your travels and have your emissions calculated. It takes into account the consumption of the vehicle and, of course, the specific distance you drove.

Our vision

Why should you use our emission calculator permanently?
Quite simply, in order for everyone to make a difference, the first step is to raise awareness. And that's exactly our goal: to raise the awareness of each individual through summaries and statistics and thus inspire a change in many. This is the only way to specifically save emissions and contribute to saving our planet.
